J 12

Inspired by the silhouettes of racing yachts from the America’s Cup, the J12 is an icon of 21st-century watchmaking. Highly resistant ceramic, available in brilliant black or intense white. An enduring and timeless watch.

DUAL NATIONALITY

Designed in Paris, Place Vendôme. Engineered in La Chaux-de-Fonds, Switzerland.

The J12 was designed in Paris by the Watch Creation Studio at Place Vendôme, then manufactured and assembled by the CHANEL Manufacture at Chaux-de-Fonds, the birthplace of Swiss watchmaking.

EXPERTISE

THE BIG REVEAL

It took 6 years to create the Caliber 12.1. High-performing and resistant, this self-winding movement manufactured exclusively for CHANEL and chronometer-certified by the Swiss Official Chronometer Control is visible through a sapphire crystal caseback.

MORE

THE WATCH WITH 100 HANDS

The manufacturing of the J12 is extremely detailed work, requiring the intervention of 50 expert watchmakers throughout the entire process, from design to production. A unique savoir-faire for a unique piece.

MORE

THE BLUEPRINT

The CHANEL Watch Creation Studio drawings are studied closely by experts to ensure they follow international standards and product viability. Then, they are modeled in their entirety.

DETAILS

70 HOURS OF SLEEP

The chronometer-certified Caliber 12.1 with a power reserve of up to 70 hours. The wrist’s movement provides the energy needed for the watch to work. When it’s not worn, the energy generated by that movement allows for a battery life of 70 hours.

A SOUND APPROACH

Hours of acoustic research went into finding the unique sound for the J12 bezel. Specifically defined by Arnaud Chastaingt, Director of the CHANEL Watch Creation Studio, the sound of the J12 is sweet, light and hushed. The sound of CHANEL.
